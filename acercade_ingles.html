It is the best place to buy video games

If you are a videogame passionate , you surely know everything about Steam. It is one of the platforms with a broader catalog of titles today. In addition, with Steam OS and a Steam Machine you can have the best possible PC machine to play with: easy, powerful and versatile.

To play on Steam, you just have to create an account and download Steam as detailed above. To be able to play without problems you must meet these minimum system requirements on your PC, although they are quite affordable for any average computer today nowadays.

  • Windows XP, Vista o 7 (also compatible with Linux Ubuntu)
  • 512 MB RAM
  • 1 GHz processor or higher
  • Steam is also available on Mac computers. These are the minimum system requirements:
  • Mac Intel, OS X version 10.7 (Lion) or later
  • A two button mouse is highly recommended
  • GB of hard disk space (recommended)
  • Internet connection (broadband recommend)
